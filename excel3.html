Build Excel Interactive Dashboards

  • Intent

    • The dashboard is applied in the data of sales data sample in excel format. The data set can check in this link. The data contains information about the sale of company goods such as the date ordered, the product ordered and the party who made the order. Details of the data are explained in the description of the data section.

  • Description of the data

    • This dataset contains the following data:

    # Column Detail
    1 OrderNum Unique ID
    2 QuantityOrdered Many items ordered
    3 PriceEach Item unit price
    4 Sales Final selling price
    5 OrderDate The date the item was ordered
    6 QTR_ID Quarter date ordered
    7 Month_ID Items ordered in the month
    8 Year_ID Items ordered in the year
    9 ProductLine Type of goods ordered
    10 ProductCode Ordered item code
    11 CustomerName The name of the person who placed the order
    12 Country Origin country of the customer
    13 DealSize Ordered item size

    Shape of dataset (2824, 13)

  • Making Pivot Table

    • Knowing the number of items ordered by a country in a certain year

    Knowing the number of goods ordered by a country in a certain size

    Knowing the selling price in a certain month and year

    Knowing the total selling price of products in a country for a certain year

    • The dashboard is made dynamic, with the table view on the left is the determining category and the right is the visualization image
